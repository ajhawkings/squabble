We are Squabble, a Young Enterprise team

We are a group of 9 students representing King's College School at the UK's Young Enterprise competition in 2023
We've come up with an idea for a game that's both fun and educational. It's called Squabble!
It encourages people to all ages to think about an issue and debate with other characters in the game.
But... there's a twist. You're debating as a fictional character and you have to argue it from their point of view.
You see - it really is both fun and educational... That's why it's so seriously silly!

- Squabble Young Enterprise team

Tips for debating

  • Do not interrupt while others are speaking
  • Listen carefully to fellow players
  • No idea is stupid!
  • Keep it chill - don't raise your voice!
  • Always be calm and polite to other players

Why debate?

Debate is an integral part of our modern day life, in the form of both personal interactions and public affairs. It is a great way to express opposing viewpoints and, through respectful dialogue, learn to understand the perspective of someone with different ideas. Through a combination of precise instructions and illustrative examples, we can learn how to sharpen our debating skills and become more confident public speakers.  Read more about this
