We are Squabble, a Young Enterprise team

We are a group of 9 students representing King's College School at the UK's Young Enterprise competition in 2022
We've come up with an idea for a game that's both fun and educational. It's called Squabble!
It encourages people to all ages to think about an issue and debate with other characters in the game.
But... there's a twist. You're debating as a fictional character and you have to argue it from their point of view.
You see - it really is both fun and educational... That's why it's so seriously silly!

- Squabble Young Enterprise team
